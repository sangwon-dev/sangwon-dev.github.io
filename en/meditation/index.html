How to Meditate

Follow these steps to start your meditation:

  1. Find a comfortable seated or lying position.
  2. Breathe deeply and relax your mind and body.
  3. Focus on your breathing. Pay attention only to your inhales and exhales.
  4. If your mind wanders, return your attention to your breathing.
  5. When the meditation time is up, slowly open your eyes.
